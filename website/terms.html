Terms & Conditions

Last updated July 2026. Plain-language basics for using this site and booking Jessie's for an event.

This page covers the general terms of using this website and requesting a booking. It's a general reference, not a substitute for the specific written quote and any agreement you receive when you book an event — those documents govern the details of your particular event.

Using this website

This site is provided for general information about Jessie's — our syrups, coffee & matcha bar, menu, pricing, and how to book us for an event. We keep it accurate to the best of our ability, but details like pricing, flavors, and market schedules can change season to season. If something on the site seems out of date, reach out and we'll confirm the current details.

Quotes & booking

Submitting the event inquiry form is a request for a quote, not a confirmed booking. Nothing is charged automatically, and no date is held until you receive a written quote and confirm it with us. Final pricing, guest minimums, deposit requirements, and cancellation or rescheduling terms are set out in that written quote for your specific event.

Cottage food disclosure

Our small-batch syrups are made in a home kitchen under the Colorado Cottage Foods Act. That kitchen is not inspected by the State of Colorado, which is standard and legal for cottage food producers operating under this law. Syrups are made, labeled, and sold in accordance with that Act. See our FAQ page for more on what the Cottage Foods Act covers.

Allergens & dietary needs

We're happy to talk through ingredients and accommodate dietary preferences where we can, but our syrups and drinks are prepared in a kitchen and cart that also handles common allergens (including dairy and tree nuts), and we can't guarantee any product is completely free of trace allergens. If you or your guests have a serious allergy, please let us know in advance so we can talk through options.

Mobile cart service

The coffee & matcha cart operates as a licensed mobile food unit and carries general liability insurance; a certificate of insurance is available for venues on request. Service is subject to reasonable venue access, power, and space requirements discussed during booking, and to safe operating conditions (severe weather, venue safety issues, etc. may require adjusting or rescheduling service).
