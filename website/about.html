About me

Jessie

Hi! I'm Jessie, and I've fallen head over heels for making really good coffee — and matcha, and syrups, and pretty much anything that comes in a cup. I used to think I just didn't like coffee. Turns out I'd just never had it made with real care. Quality really does make the difference!

Jessies. started as small syrup batches for friends and turned into a farmers market table, and now a full mobile coffee & matcha bar for weddings and corporate events. Whatever's in your cup, I want it to feel a little playful, a little indulgent, and made just for you.

Coffee should be fun. Life's better one sip at a time — I can't wait to pour you one. 🤍
