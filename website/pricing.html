Good to know

Under the minimum?

Smaller gatherings are still welcome — they're typically billed as a flat event minimum rather than per person. Ask for a quote either way.

Extra time

Need more than the booked window? Additional hours can usually be added at a flat hourly add-on rate.

Big guest counts

Events over ~150 guests may need a second barista for smooth service — this gets factored into your quote.

Looking for syrups instead?

Bottled syrups are sold separately at $12 each — see the full flavor menu.
